Oven Baked fish

Easy recipe for people who spent so much on the fish they couldn't buy the sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 (6 ounce) salmon steaks
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Method of Cooking

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  2. In a small bowl, mix honey, mustard, and lemon juice.

    Spread the mixture over the salmon steaks. Season with pepper. Arrange in a medium baking dish.

  3. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until fish easily flakes with a fork.
